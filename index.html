cake

It's cake, pretty good. Very light a fluffy. Gordan Ramsay is jealous.

ingredients

  • 3 and 2/3 cups (420g) cake flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 and 1/2 cups (345g) unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
  • 2 cups (400g) granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs + 2 additional egg whites, at room temperature*

direction

Put the wet ingridents togther and mix in a large bowl, then set aside. Grab your dry ingredients and sift it in a bowl together, after mixing the dry ingredients together, in portions start mixing the dry and wet ingridents together. When done mixing set aside and pre-heat your oven for 375 degrees and bake for about 25 minutes. Let it cool, by letting it sit in the fridge for an hour before applying your pre-made frosting and decorations. Enjoy!
